Evolve the Pokemon

When the pokemon is clicked the first time, it will evolve to the 2nd pokemon

When the pokemon is clicked a second time, it will evolve to the 3rd pokemon

Evolution will stop at the third pokemon

Squirtle Evolves to Wartortle. Wartortle evolves to Blastoise

Bulbasaurs Evolves to Ivysaur. Ivysaur evolves to Venusaur
