Some facts about Quentin Tarantino

  • Tarantino considered being a novelist before making films.
  • He attended the James Best Acting School
  • He once went to his own movie and asked the movie theatre if he could get in for free since he made the movie.
  • Quentin Tarantino owns the Chevelle Malibu that appears in Pulp Fiction.
